About Me

I am Yan Ting Leung, also known as Joey or Ennil online. I am currently a linguistics student in Simon Fraser University enrolled in computing science minor who is learning software development to achieve my dream -- to make games.

How do I end up here

I was born in Hong Kong in 2003 and finished my high school there. By that time there were still frequent protesting in Hong Kong, and my family, holding opinions against all teenagers in sight, would often have political opinions at the dinning table and starting heated arguments. Seeking for a quiet place to continue my studying and life, I planned to study abord, and I eventually made it into Simon Fraser University, in BC, Canada.
